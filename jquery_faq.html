Top Ten Most Visited National Parks in the US

Great Smoky Mountains
Most visited national park in the US, with 11 million visitors in on year.
Grand Canyon
The Grand Canyon is 277 miles long being up to 1 mile deep and 18 miles wide at some points.
Zion
Filled with rock towers, sandstone canyons, and sharp cliffs.
Rocky Mountain
The park contains 150 lakes and 450 miles of streams.
Yosemite
This mile-wide, 7-mile-long canyon was cut by a river and then widened and deepened by glacial action.
Yellowstone
Yellowstone is the world's first national park.
Acadia National Park
Located in Maine on Mount Desert Island.
Olympic
No roads cross through the park. It contains a temperate rain forest, wildflower meadow, and rugged Pacific shore.
Grand Teton
The peaks stand nearly 7,000 feet above the Wyoming valley floor.
Glacier
The park's Going-to-the-Sun Road is considered by many to be one of the world's most spectacular drives.
